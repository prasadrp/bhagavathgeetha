About Us

Bhagavad Gita brings answers for all the multifaceted questions regarding life, sins and eternity. It is a must-read for all around the world irrespective of their caste, creed, race, ethnicity and gender. There is always a myth about the readability of Gita as it is written during ancient times and filled with Sanskrit Slokas. Mithra media is here to make it simple and reachable to everyone. We are here to give Bhagavad Gita Audio Book narrated musically in the simplest language. Along with Slokas recited in its pure musical way we narrate the meaning of whole 18 chapters. The meanings are explained in the simplest way possible. We use 18 different ragas to 18 chapters according to their tone and mood which gives a total blissful experience. We use the language in a way that the entire Malayali population regardless of their social strata should understand it.

It is true that Bhagavad Gita is a mandatory spiritual experience one should feel. Our Bhagavad Gita audiobook is a humble attempt to reach out the essence of this great spiritual awakening and literary classic.
